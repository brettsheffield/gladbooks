Line Text Macros

Back to Help Menu

Some linetext macros are available for use in either the Product Description field, or, when creating Sales Orders, in the Line Text when adding products to an order.

Each of these macros is replaced with generated text when a Sales Invoice is generated. The following macros are available:

NB: all dates are calculated based on the taxpoint of the invoice unless stated otherwise

%%%DAY%%% the day of the month (01 - 31)
%%%MONTH%%% The full name of the month (eg. January)
%%%YEAR%%% The four-digit year (eg. 2013)
%%%MONTH-n%%% (where n is an integer between 1 and 2) As %%%MONTH%%%, but skip back n months.
%%%MONTH+n%%% (where n is an integer between 1 and 12) As %%%MONTH%%%, but skip forward n months.
%%%PERIOD%%% 'YYYY-MM-DD to YYYY-MM-DD'. For use with recurring Sales Orders. The first date is the tax point, and the second date is the day before the tax point of the next recurring invoice.
